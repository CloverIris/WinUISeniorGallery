# Settings Flyout Design Archaeology and Modernization

Retain temporary settings layers without leaving the task, clear title/back, and hierarchical settings. Reject hiding settings only at a system edge, panels without a close exit, and forcing a full form into narrow width. Modern `SettingsPanel` needs an explicit app discovery point, logical-end placement on wide windows, and bottom/center-page fallback when narrow.

Light/Dark/High Contrast distinguish title, separators, and focus; RTL uses logical end; 100/150/200% DPI and text scaling cannot clip. Reduced Motion presents immediately; Tab order is entry, title, back, settings, close; Esc closes one layer; Narrator announces hierarchy, unsaved state, and failure reason.
