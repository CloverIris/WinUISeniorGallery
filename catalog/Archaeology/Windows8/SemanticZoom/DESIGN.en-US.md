# Semantic Zoom Design Archaeology and Modernization

Retain the idea that overview is a navigation level and returning preserves position context. Reject equating animated scaling with semantic navigation, pinch-only access, and unlabelled thumbnails. The modern owner needs explicit reachable overview/detail entry and readable group labels.

Narrow windows use a switch button, not pinch; regular/wide windows may explain side-by-side. Light/Dark/High Contrast distinguish selected group, focus, and text; RTL ordering follows language/data; 100/150/200% DPI cannot clip indexes. Reduced Motion changes hierarchy immediately; Tab, arrows, and Narrator report level, group, and return target.
