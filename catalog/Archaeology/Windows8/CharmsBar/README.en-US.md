# Charms Bar (Windows 8 Design Archaeology)

## Position and boundary

This exhibit studies the Windows 8 (2012) Charms Bar: Search, Share, Start, Devices, and Settings collected at the right edge. It is not a system-level recreation and owns no stable API. The modern in-app owner is `windowing.edge-command-panel` (`EdgeCommandPanel`). This folder records historical information architecture, retained design DNA, and rejected system assumptions only.
