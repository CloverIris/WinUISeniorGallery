## Input, Window, and Lifecycle

Demo belongs to current Gallery XamlRoot and creates/migrates no Window. Touch upward drag or ellipsis expands; keyboard/gamepad reaches every command; Escape/B closes by layer and restores trigger/content focus. Unload cancels animation, delay, and Provider, ignores late results, and restores navigation focus.
