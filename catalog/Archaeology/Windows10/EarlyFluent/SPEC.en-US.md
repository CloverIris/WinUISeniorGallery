## Design DNA

Material layering, pointer-proximity feedback, spatial continuity, content depth, and restrained motion.

## Modern boundary

RevealBorderBehavior and ConnectedTransitionBehavior extract interaction principles only; they copy no Shell assets, and Acrylic/Mica must follow current system resources and accessibility fallbacks.

## Non-goals

Do not invent history for unshipped work or describe a Gallery simulation as Windows Shell, a system service, or stable API.
