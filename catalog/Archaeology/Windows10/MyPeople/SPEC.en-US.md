## Design DNA

A bounded frequent-people set, avatar anchors, quick actions, recent context, and cross-app sharing.

## Modern boundary

Reconstruct only an in-app PeopleCard/recent-people surface; never modify the taskbar or read system contacts/messages, and require host-supplied personal data.

## Non-goals

Do not invent history for unshipped work or describe a Gallery simulation as Windows Shell, a system service, or stable API.
