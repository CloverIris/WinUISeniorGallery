## Design DNA

Mixed-content tabs, task grouping, session restoration, and tab tear-out into a new window.

## Modern boundary

The reconstruction is an app-owned TabbedShell only; it must not embed arbitrary external app windows, claim OS-level tabs, or present unshipped behavior as final.

## Non-goals

Do not invent history for unshipped work or describe a Gallery simulation as Windows Shell, a system service, or stable API.
